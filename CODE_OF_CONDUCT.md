# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to keeping Forsetti Framework - Windows welcoming, respectful, and constructive for everyone participating in the project.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using respectful and inclusive language
- Focusing feedback on framework quality, safety, and maintainability
- Welcoming questions about setup, architecture, and guardrails without condescension
- Accepting constructive criticism and clarifying disagreements professionally

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Racist, hateful, vulgar, obscene, or profane language
- Threats, intimidation, or encouragement of self-harm
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional engineering space

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer. Violating content may be removed from project spaces, and contributors may be restricted or banned when behavior is severe or repeated. Project discussions may also be moderated automatically against these standards.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and GitHub Discussions.
